Wānaka Lavender Farm

Must · View · Shop
36 Morris Rd · 6 min from airport · Opens 9:00

20 acres of lavender, alpacas and pigs, gift shop with honey + lavender skincare, and the (genuinely good) lavender ice-cream. Mid-November is shoulder bloom — full purple peaks late Dec, but you'll catch first colour with no crowds. Self-guided, ~1 hr is plenty.

#ThatWānakaTree

Must · Photo Stop
Roys Bay foreshore · 5-min flat walk from car park

The lone willow growing out of Lake Wānaka — the most photographed tree in NZ. Park along Lakeside Rd, walk west along the shore. Morning light is much softer than midday. Quick stop only.

13:00 45 min

Rippon Vineyard — Tasting

Must · Winery
246 Mt Aspiring Rd · BOOK 13:00 SLOT in advance · Free

Sits between lunch in town and the hike trailhead — a 7-min drive from each. Biodynamic, family-run since 1912, 6 wine varieties, and arguably the most cinematic vineyard view on earth: Lake Wānaka stretching below, Southern Alps behind. Tastings are 30 min, max 6 people, by appointment only — booking is mandatory at rippon.co.nz. Free unless you upgrade to the $45 private tour. Designated driver only — 6-berth motorhome rule.

Open in Google Maps
14:00 3 hrs

Diamond Lake & Rocky Mountain Track

Must · Hike
Mt Aspiring Rd · 15 min from Rippon (same road) · 7.3 km loop · ~520 m ascent

The smarter alternative to Roy's Peak: shorter, quieter, every bit as scenic. Continue west on the same road you took from Rippon — no doubling back. Three layered viewpoints stack on top of each other:

  • 🌿 Diamond Lake Lookout — 1 hr return, easy
  • 🏔 Lake Wānaka Lookout — 2 hr return, moderate (bench at the top)
  • ⛰ Rocky Mountain Summit — 3 hr return, 775 m, full 360° payoff

Recommendation: go up the East track for views, summit Rocky Mountain, descend via the West track — no backtrack. Bring water + windproof. Sandflies at the summit.

Cellar Door Decision

Three vineyards, one stop.
Here's why Rippon wins on a Wānaka day.

Rippon

246 Mt Aspiring Rd · 10 min west of town
  • Drive 10 min from town
  • Tasting FREE (30 min · max 6)
  • View ★★★★★ Lake + Alps
  • Hours 11–17 daily, by appt
  • Vibe Biodynamic, family
  • Best for Pinot Noir, Riesling
  • Detour ZERO — on the way back from Diamond Lake
"Possibly the most picturesque cellar door in the world." Already on your route — no backtrack, no detour, free.

Nanny Goat

68 Queensberry Terrace · 15 min south-east
  • Drive 20 min from town
  • Tasting $15 (waived w/ purchase)
  • View ★★★ Vines + Clutha
  • Hours 11–17 (Closed Tue/Wed)
  • Vibe Pinot specialist + grazing
  • Best for Pinot Noir, Cross Breed
  • Detour Wrong direction from hike — adds 30 min
Best wines of the three (Pinot magic), but the detour kills it on a packed day.

Aitken's Folly

246 Riverbank Rd · 10 min south-east
  • Drive 12 min from town
  • Tasting Modest fee (waived w/ purchase)
  • View ★★ Pleasant, no lake
  • Hours 12–17 daily
  • Vibe Tiny, unpretentious, host-led
  • Best for Blanc de Noir, Chardonnay
  • Detour Doable but opens at 12:00 — clashes with lunch
Charming hidden gem (highest TripAdvisor score, 4.9), but no view, no late open, no convenience.
