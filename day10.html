🛶 Focused day — one main objective, generous margins

Today is built around the 5pm Sunset Kayak on Lake Mapourika. Everything else is a relaxed Haast Pass scenic drive with multiple optional stops. No Fox Glacier detour today.

  • 90 min buffer before kayak check-in — relaxed arrival.
  • Full Blue Pools walk and proper Hard Antler lunch — no rushing.
  • Reception still open when you check in to Top 10.
  • Sleep in tomorrow — no dawn alarm needed.

🌅 Morning · Wānaka & Haast Pass

07:00 Depart Hampshire Holiday Park Wānaka —

Pre-pack last night. Empty grey/black water, top up fresh. Quick breakfast in the van.

📍 Open in Maps
07:10 ⛽ McKeown CardFuel 24/7 — FILL FULL TANK Must15 min

Cheapest diesel for hundreds of km. Open 24/7. Fill to brim — next cheap fuel is Hokitika (Day 11 evening). Avoid Haast/Fox/Franz Josef pumps.

📍 73 Ballantyne Rd
07:30 ☕ Pembroke Patisserie — Breakfast & Coffee Must30 min

Opens 07:30 (Monday confirmed). Famous almond croissants. Sit down if you have time, otherwise takeaway. Stock up on extra pastries for snacks on the road.

📍 20 Alison Ave
08:30 📷 Lake Wānaka Lookout Optional10 min

Quick scenic pull-off above Lake Wānaka along SH6.

📍 Open in Maps
08:45 📷 The Neck — Lake Hawea Lookout Must20 min

Iconic twin-lake panorama from the isthmus between Lake Wānaka and Lake Hawea. Take time for photos here — morning light is excellent.

📍 Open in Maps
09:40 ☕ Makarora Country Cafe Optional20 min

Last proper toilet + coffee before Haast Pass. Charming old-school cafe with a fireplace. Card-only emergency pump on-site.

📍 5507 Makarora-Lake Hawea Rd
10:30 💧 Blue Pools Walk Must60 min

1.5 km return through beech forest, over two swing bridges to electric-blue glacial pools. Bring towel if anyone wants to brave the cold dip. Now you have time to do it properly.

📍 Open in Maps
11:45 💦 Fantail Falls Recommended15 min

2-min walk from carpark to a delicate fan-shaped waterfall. Easy stretch break.

📍 Open in Maps
12:05 🌉 Gates of Haast Bridge Optional5 min

Drive-by photo of the roaring Haast River rapids. Pull off only if safe — no formal carpark.

📍 Open in Maps
12:15 💧 Thunder Creek Falls Must20 min

5-min walk to a 28m single-drop waterfall plunging into a gorge. Best waterfall on the Haast Pass route — many say the best stop between Wānaka and Franz Josef.

📍 Open in Maps

🍴 Midday · Haast & West Coast Drive

13:30 🍔 The Hard Antler, Haast — LUNCH Must60 min

Best meal between Wānaka and Franz Josef. Try the whitebait sandwich (West Coast specialty) or venison burger. Open 11am–10pm. Toilets across road. Big garlic butter on every steak.

📍 5 Marks Rd, Haast
14:30 🏛️ Awarua/Haast Visitor Centre Optional20 min

Small but well-curated museum on West Coast history & ecology. DOC staff can confirm SH6 road conditions ahead. Clean toilets.

📍 Open in Maps
15:15 🌊 Knights Point Lookout Must20 min

First sweeping Tasman Sea view — dramatic rocky coastline and rolling forested headlands. Public toilets, large carpark. Good leg-stretch.

📍 Open in Maps
15:50 🌳 Ship Creek Walk Optional30 min

Choose one: the sand dune walk along the Tasman, or the kahikatea swamp boardwalk through ancient forest. Both are short and stunning.

📍 Open in Maps

🏕️ Late Afternoon · Arrive Franz Josef

16:30 🏕️ Check in: Franz Josef Top 10 Holiday Park Reception open20 min

Reception open until 17:00 — you arrive comfortably in time. Get site number, plug in motorhome, sort gear before kayak. Mountain & rainforest setting.

📍 2902 Franz Josef Highway

🛶 Evening · Sunset Kayak (Main Objective)

16:45 🛶 Franz Josef Wilderness Tours — Check-in Main objective15 min early

Meet at 30 Main Road for the 5pm Sunset Classic Kayak. Use the buffer to use bathroom, layer up, leave valuables in motorhome. Walk over from Top 10 (~12 min) or drive (~3 min, parking on Main Rd).

📍 30 Main Rd
17:00 🌅 Sunset Classic Kayak — Lake Mapourika Main objective3 hrs

Transfer to Lake Mapourika (~10 min drive). Glide across glassy water with Southern Alps and glaciers reflected. Paddle into the Ōkārito Kiwi Sanctuary edge. Sunset around 20:30 — golden hour over the alps. Wet weather gear provided. Call +64 3 752 0230 if delayed.

📍 Booking office
20:30 🍜 SnakeBite Brewery — Late Dinner Tight60 min

Closes 20:30 — last orders before 20:00. Two safer options:
① Pre-order by phone during your kayak transfer back (+64 3 752 0234) so food is ready.
② Cook in motorhome — pull pre-prepped pasta/curry from fridge for a faster evening.
Asian fusion menu: panang curry, nasi goreng, calamari salad.

📍 28 Main Rd
