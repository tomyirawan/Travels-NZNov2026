How to pay for the bus

Christchurch launched Motu Move on 10 Nov 2025 — just tap a contactless debit/credit card, Apple Pay or Google Pay on the reader by the driver. Flat NZ$3 per trip with free transfers within 2 hours. Cash is still accepted (NZ$4.20, exact change preferred).

The Route 29 stop is right outside International Arrivals, Door 9 (next to the Novotel) — a 2-min walk from the terminal. Buses run every 30 min.

The civic heart. See the ongoing reinstatement of ChristChurch Cathedral (damaged in 2011, currently being rebuilt), the Chalice sculpture, and street art down the laneways. Photo stop, not a long one.

Free20 min
Two-minute walk north. A pedestrianised lane of pastel-pink Spanish Mission storefronts from 1932, with the heritage tram clattering through. The most photogenic street in the city. Pop into Rollickin' Gelato if dinner can wait.

Free15 minCloses 18:00 Sat — exterior only
A 5-min walk east. Designed by Shigeru Ban using 98 cardboard tubes — the world's only cardboard cathedral. By 7 PM Saturday it'll be closed; the silhouette against the dusk light is still striking. Skip if you'd rather head straight to dinner.

⛽ Diesel & Fuel Notes

Day 1 fuel usage: 0 litres / NZ$0. You're on public transport — no vehicle in play yet.

For when you pick up a diesel vehicle (Day 2+ prep):

  • Christchurch is typically the cheapest fuel city in NZ. Expect diesel around NZ$1.85–1.95/L retail in Nov 2026 (recent range NZ$1.86–1.96).
  • Cheapest near the airport — typically NPD Russley (~3 km south, 542 Russley Rd) or Gull Russley. Use the free Gaspy app on arrival for live prices — usually 15–30 c/L below BP/Z/Mobil.
  • High-flow diesel pumps (for campervans / large rigs): Allied 24/7 Truckstop, Waterloo Road, Hornby (about 10 min from the airport) and NPD Truckstop Waterloo Rd / Hornby — both unmanned, 24/7, drive-through with AdBlue at the pump. Bring a payment card the rental company has authorised.
  • Don't forget RUC on diesel vehicles — currently ~NZ$0.076/km (rental companies usually charge ~NZ$0.08–0.09/km). Budget this separately from pump price.
  • Fill near Christchurch before heading inland — Tekapo, Te Anau, Franz Josef and Milford run 20–30 c/L higher.
